## üíé Gem of the Week

[Colored Tags](https://github.com/pfrankov/obsidian-colored-tags) by pfrankov is a plugin that allows you to customize the color of your tags in Obsidian. You can set different colors for different tags, making it easier to visually organize and identify them in your notes. It's a great way to add a personal touch to your tagging system and improve the overall aesthetics of your notes.

## üîå Plugins

### New Plugins

- [Task Zero: A keyboard-first GTD task management plugin](https://forum.obsidian.md/t/task-zero-a-keyboard-first-gtd-task-management-plugin/108649)
- [DOCX Exporter](https://obsidian.md/plugins?id=docx-exporter): Help users easily export note content into DOCX format.
- [Fix Math](https://obsidian.md/plugins?id=fix-math): Convert LaTeX math delimiters to Obsidian format.
- [OCR Extractor](https://obsidian.md/plugins?id=ocr-extractor): Extract text from documents, images, etc. and store it as Markdown in your notes.
- [Reading Time](https://obsidian.md/plugins?id=obsidian-reading-time): Add an estimated reading time for the selected note to the StatusBar.
- [AI Companion](https://obsidian.md/plugins?id=ai-companion): An AI-powered assistant for Obsidian.

### üíé Gem of the Week

Colored Tags https://github.com/pfrankov/obsidian-colored-tags by pfrankov is a plugin that allows you to customize the color of your tags in Obsidian. You can set different colors for different tags, making it easier to visually organize and identify them in your notes. It's a great way to add a personal touch to your tagging system and improve the overall aesthetics of your notes.
